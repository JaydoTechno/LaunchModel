Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

LaunchModel ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use LaunchModel at launchmodel.ai.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Email address — if you voluntarily provide it through our email capture form
  • Quiz responses — your answers to the assessment questions (stored locally in your browser, not on our servers)
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics about how users interact with the site (pages visited, time spent)

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you your personalized business model results and related content
  • To improve the LaunchModel assessment and user experience
  • To send occasional updates, tips, or offers relevant to building an online business (you can unsubscribe at any time)

How We Store Your Information

Your quiz answers are stored only in your browser's local storage and are never transmitted to our servers. Your email address, if provided, is stored securely with our email service provider.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Gumroad — for payment processing. Gumroad's privacy policy applies to all purchases.
  • Vercel — for hosting. Vercel may collect standard server logs.
  • Google Fonts — for typography. Google may collect usage data per their privacy policy.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for site functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or sell your data to advertisers.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your email address or any personal data we hold at any time by contacting us at support@launchmodel.ai.

Children's Privacy

LaunchModel is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
